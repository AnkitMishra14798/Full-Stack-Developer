WELCOME TO OUR   SR GROUP OF INSTITUTIONS

Subedar Rai Devi Educational Trust founded in the year 2008 is registered under the society registration act of 1860. In 2010 S R Group of Institutions came into existance with establishment of S R M Business School for MBA programme with 120 seats.
